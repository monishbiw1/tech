From Aisle Match to a Promise in April 2027

A journey that began on 22 July 2025, grew through late-night talks, moving boxes, birthday memories, and turns into a beautiful, mutual “yes” in April 2027.

Our Timeline

July 2025 • The Beginning

We matched on Aisle on 22 July 2025 and met on 26 July at Fursat Ke Pal. We stayed till 4:30 AM under the sky, talking deeply and laughing over a cheeky alphabet game.

August 2025 • New Ashok Nagar to Vaishali

In the first week itself, life moved fast. She shifted from New Ashok Nagar to Vaishali, and we packed everything together—boxes, clothes, and all the details of her world.

Before that move, at her New Ashok Nagar flat, I gifted her a 30-piece earrings set. While setting up her Vaishali room, I also got curtains for her place.

Five Days That Changed Us

When office trouble unexpectedly gave me five leaves, I stayed at her room for those full five days. She left for office each morning and returned each evening—those days became our real bonding chapter.

Dates, Birthdays & Friends

We had a sky-view dinner date with lots of pictures. Before her 3-day Lansdowne girls trip, I made her birthday special with a wall poster of her pictures, chocolates, sweets, and cookies.

Her birthday night was celebrated at her room with Manju, and on the weekend we went to Fursat Ke Pal with her friends—Lakhan, Priya, Divya, Geeta, and others.

February 2026 • Valentine Week

On Chocolate Day, I gifted her chocolates. On Rose Day, she gave me a rose and chocolate. This year, Valentine’s plans are at my room—with a clear conversation about balance, effort, and showing up for each other equally.

2026 to April 2027 • Choosing Forever

Through 2026, we grow by communicating better, respecting each other’s space, sharing responsibilities, and supporting each other’s goals. We keep building memories—small dates, festivals, family introductions, and trust.

In early 2027, both families meet. There are disagreements and stressful moments, but we stay patient, kind, and united. No drama can break what we build with honesty.

April 2027: We marry with full consent, joy, and blessings—because we chose each other again and again, every single day.

Our Promise

Love is not “no matter what happens.” Love is “whatever happens, we handle it together with respect.” This is our story of effort, care, and a future we build side by side.
